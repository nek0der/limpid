# Support

How to reach Limpid:

Before filing, search existing [Issues](https://github.com/nek0der/limpid/issues) and [Discussions](https://github.com/nek0der/limpid/discussions), and skim the [README](README.md) and [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) — your question may already be answered.

- **Bug reports** — open a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/nek0der/limpid/issues). One reproducible bug per issue. Include your macOS version, hardware (Apple Silicon model), and the exact action that triggered it.
- **Ideas, questions, "how do I"** — start a [Discussion](https://github.com/nek0der/limpid/discussions).
- **Security disclosures** — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Do not file security issues in public Issues or Discussions.

Limpid is maintained by one person; expect responses on a developer cadence, not a vendor SLA.
